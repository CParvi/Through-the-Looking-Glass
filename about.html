Through the Looking Glass  

Hi, my name is Clayton and I'm here to give you a peek through my lens. What the world looks like through the eyes of any photographer is unique. Allow me to give you an example of what that looks like.

Life really is what you make it. Ever since I recieved my first phone, I have been intrigued with the art of taking photos. Perfection or special effects aren't what really matters, it's about capturing that unique moment. I would take photos of flora, clouds, the night sky, or anything that would fascinate me. Now that I have my own camera and know a little bit more about taking pictures, I enjoy it even more. Here you will find a taste of what I've found.
